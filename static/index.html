          Real-time multilingual transcription

          Your meetings,
          understood in every language

          Meeting Scribe captures live audio, transcribes in real time, and translates between any pair of 10 supported languages, all running locally on your hardware.

          1

          Set up the room

          Choose a table layout and add seats for each participant. Optionally enroll speakers by recording a short voice sample so the system can identify who is speaking.

          2

          Start the meeting

          Hit "Start Meeting" and speak normally. Audio is captured from your device microphone and the live transcript shows each utterance in its original language with the translation shown alongside.

          3

          Speak in any supported language

          The system automatically detects the spoken language. Each utterance is transcribed in its original language and translated to the paired language in real time. Speaker identification labels who said what.

          4

          Review when finished

          When you stop the meeting, an AI summary is generated with topics, decisions, action items, and speaker participation stats. Download the transcript as Markdown, plain text, or a ZIP archive.
